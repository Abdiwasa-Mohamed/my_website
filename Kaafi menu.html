Best Restaurants and Bars in Nairobi

Overlooking the Nairobi National Park

Created with Artistry & Driven by Taste

Helmed by Group Executive Chef Torsten Schubert and executed by a team of trained chefs and mixologists, dining at 2.5.4 Ole Sereni offers an exploration of inventive global cuisine as told through rich flavors and long-standing traditions. Each of our dining options and top restaurants in Nairobi is backdropped by the iconic Nairobi National Park in a sumptuous setting. Whether you are enjoying your favorite cocktail at Ngong Pool Bar, savoring hand-picked aged steaks or fresh seafood at Eagle’s The Steakhouse, indulging in Indian delicacies at the Big Five Restaurant, or trying specialty sandwiches at Waterhole Snack Bar, the traditional flavors come alive in vibrant dishes with breathtaking views.

EAGLE'S STEAKHOUSE

Eagle’s the Steakhouse is the most exclusive restaurant in Nairobi. Where the chirruping of birds and the bray of zebras make it subtle yet soothing for local and global travelers. Take a whiff of the finest aged steaks that are hand-picked, relish fresh seafood from Kenya’s coast, and experience the mouth-watering vegetarian options that will satisfy your taste buds and warm your heart. Located on the fourth floor with its private bar, artisanal lounge, and one of the best private dining rooms in Nairobi, it is ideal to host pre-dinner drinks or reception overlooking the waterhole in Nairobi National Park, Ngong Hills, and the stunning skyline.

It is now open till 10:30 pm.

BIG FIVE RESTAURANT

Named after the Big Five animals of Africa – lion, leopard, rhino, elephant, and African buffalo, it is one of the best fine dining restaurants in Nairobi. Located on the first floor of 2.5.4 Ole Sereni, it is set on the large veranda to enjoy a direct view of our animal watering hole, where buffalos and other wildlife are frequent visitors. Gaze at the exquisite views of the Nairobi National Park while savoring a la carte lunch favorites and traditional Indian dinner, bottomless sangrias, teas, coffees, shakes, and more at our versatile open-show kitchen.

It is now open till 10:30 pm.
